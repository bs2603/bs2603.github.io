Motion Planning of Legged Robots using RL


This project has started recently, I am researching on the motion planning of different legged robots on legged terrains. I am working on this project under the guidance of professor R.Warrier of my university , BITS Goa.
This project aims to explore motion of different legged Robots, and study different gaits.
Moreover the research aims to use Reinforcement learning to simulate a bot and teach it locomotion.
